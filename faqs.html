Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our bus booking service

How do I book a bus ticket?
Booking a bus ticket is simple! Just follow these steps: 1. Select your source and destination 2. Choose your travel date 3. Select from available buses 4. Choose your preferred seats 5. Fill in passenger details 6. Make payment You'll receive your ticket confirmation via email and SMS.

Still have questions? Contact Us

What payment methods are accepted?
We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI, and popular digital wallets. All payments are processed through secure payment gateways to ensure your financial information is safe.

Still have questions? Contact Us

How can I cancel my ticket?
To cancel your ticket, go to 'My Bookings', select the ticket you wish to cancel, and click on the 'Cancel Booking' button. Cancellation charges may apply based on how close to the journey date you're cancelling. Refunds typically process within 5-7 business days.

Still have questions? Contact Us

What is your refund policy?
Refund amount depends on when you cancel: - 48+ hours before departure: 100% refund - 24-48 hours before departure: 50% refund - Less than 24 hours: No refund Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5-7 business days.

Still have questions? Contact Us

Can I modify my booking?
Yes, you can modify your booking up to 24 hours before departure. Go to 'My Bookings', select your booking, and click 'Modify Booking'. You can change the travel date or passenger details. Additional charges may apply based on fare differences.

Still have questions? Contact Us

What documents do I need to carry?
Please carry: 1. Ticket confirmation (digital or printed) 2. Valid government-issued ID proof 3. Student ID (if student discount was availed) These documents are mandatory for boarding the bus.
